About

I'm Andrew Hickman, a 24 year old techie with an interest for electronics and programming. My home away from home is in the ocean. I've been Scuba Diving since I was 12 years old and obtained my Open Water Advanced Diver lincense at 15. I've dove in places such as Cozumel, Cancun, Saint Martin, and Honduras.
I'm a current Electronic Systems Engineering student at Texas A&M. Through Electronic Systems Engineering, I learned how to write code and soon after found myself hungry for more. Next thing I know, I'm spending my summer days working on Front-End Web Development and various Python side projects. During my school semesters, I typically spend my extra-curricular time working on my next personal programming project, whether that be a Reddit bot in Python or an Unreal4 video game in C++.
